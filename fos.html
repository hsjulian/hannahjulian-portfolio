FABRIC OF SOCIETY: Roundabout

By Hannah on March 2022 • 2 mins read
Scroll

//

The “Cagayan River Basin: fabric of society” in parametric model representation within the Tanza Roundabout, it will be the point of convergence particularly among those passing through the city proper from the downstream and nearby the area.

The polygonal waterfeature being bounded by the wood deck was inspired from the topography underlying the ‘inspired’ depth and elevation of the Cagayan River Basin that transpires as the lifeblood of every Cagayanos bringing forth abundant source of natural groundwaters to exceedingly large areas of agricultural lands, and come up to be the Philippines’s food basket.

However, Cagayan River Basin also made History caused by the deadly and intensified Typhoon Ulysses that has brought traumatic experience to every Cagayanos. The “fabric of society” structured in thick steel mesh parametric model gives emphasis on the backbone/structure of Cagayanos on how we diligently earned the loss and trauma back into a more bountiful community, all by the help of our kindhearted frontliners and generous neighbouring regions all over the Philippines.
