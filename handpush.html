HAND PUSH SEED Planter

By Hannah on October 2021 • 1 min read
Scroll

//

The utilization of animal-drawn for tilling is a major effective service in cultivating large farmland, and has been a successive implementation addressing the concern with regards to physical exhaustion elimination. However, this method of farming, particularly in the extent of seed planting, is immensely labor intensive neither stretches out to smallholders farmers.

In the same manner, the farming method involving the utilization of tractors as a mechanized method in cultivating small farmlands are unjustifiably cost-effective. The access of credit facilities in favour of enhancing the confidence of higher productivity in farming are visibly limited to smallholders farmers that couldnâ€™t afford a rental or a loan of the agricultural machineries. Farmers, then, go back to the traditional way of non-efficient way of farming that requires much time and physical energy inputs. This results in uneven distribution of seed placement, spacing, and depth, and the seeds might not be covered by soil properly. The uneven distribution of seeds with traditional sowing will result in seed wastage, uneven germination and uneven growth of the plant.

Hand Push Seed Planter focuses on the modernization of traditional planting by developing a machine that can enhance the cost-effectiveness and time-efficiency of smallholders farmers.

This equipment is a type of multifunctional small seed planter, which can be operated by a single person and is ideal for small farm land use. It is also hand-operated that will place the seed at accurate distance and depth, cover it with soil and provide appropriate compaction over the seeds for good germination.

Indeed, it is a smart way for farmers to reduce the time of planting and it will lessen the physical work needed. It reduces the problem encountered in manual seed planting and is suitable for farmers who cannot purchase high cost machinery for planting.
