AGRI E-WASTE SHREDDER Machine

By Hannah on October 2021 • 5 mins read
Scroll

//

The increasing levels of e-waste, low collection rates, and non-environmentally sound disposal and treatment of this waste streams pose significant risks to the environment. In the Philippines, the DENR-Environmental Management Bureau (DENR-EMB) also considers e-waste as one of the fastest growing waste streams and is a growing environmental concern worldwide.

In the same manner, construction industry faces many challenges currently that lead to affect project goal and steady growth of the economy. It deals difficulty with materials shortages amidst the growing demand. Due to large amount of concrete materials present in the construction industry, the availability of the raw material is being queried and disputed.

The leading objective of this study is to summarize the strong probable use of E-waste in the construction industry according to its properties and specifications to hereby bridge environmental protection and economic growth.

A two-layered electronic waste shredder machine is designed with the goal of safely disposing tons of electronic waste that have been causing environmental and human health issues (SDG 12). The shredded E-wastes will be formed as capsulated, coarse, or fine aggregates which are ideal for alternative construction materials.
