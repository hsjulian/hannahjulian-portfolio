FUWELL Panel

By Hannah on January 2023 • 4 mins read
Scroll

//

Solar energy is more sustainable energy source. The photovoltaic cell becomes the most popular in the last two decades. However, the main setback for solar energy is its seasonal availability as it is only best at a relatively cold and sunny environment, rather than humid and hot climate region.

Moreover, wind energy explains why it is the fastest growing energy source. Yet wind turbines do automatically have to be shut off in high winds and heavy rains to protect themselves.

The study should be carried out under the general objective which is to develop a panel that will harness rainfall energy to generate electricity by means of piezoelectric sensor, and to conduct an experimental work to investigate the effectiveness of the panel.

This Rainfall Energy Harvester: Fu-well Panel will harness rainfall energy togenerate electricity by means of piezoelectric sensor that is sensitive to mechanical vibration ensuring access to clean and sustainable renewable energy. (SDG 7)
